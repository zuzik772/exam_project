The safest way to order and pay in restaurant

Watch instruction how to use Quickit app in restaurant

How the guest does

Allow the guest to order and pay via their own smartphone.
Reduce bacterial spread and increase your turnover by 10%.

Step 1 - Get Quickit

Download the app “Quickit” and scan the QR code to see the menu.

Step 2 - Select order

Select your order. Add extra and tell how you would like your steak fried.

Step 3 - Payment

Choose payment method and follow your order.

Step 4 - More hungry?

Order more by repeating the process as many times as you like.
